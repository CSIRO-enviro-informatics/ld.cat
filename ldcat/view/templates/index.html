Longitudinal Spine of Government Functions Catalogue

This is a catalogue of datasets and models (ontologies) produced by the Longitudinal Spine of Government Functions project which ran in the 2018/19 Financial Year. The project was concerned with producing a series of integrated datasets and metadata that allowed functions of (Australian Federal) government to be mapped over time and crosswalks between difference functional mapping systems performed.

This catalogue contains only Linked Data assets which means that they can all be navidated with standardised methods and tooling. Additionally, this catalogue itself is a Linked Data system thus it to can be navigated just as its content can.

Registers

The registers of dfferent types of things in this catalogue are:

  1. All
  2. Datasets
  3. Linksets
  4. Definitional Items

Linked Data access

This catalogue is available as a Linked Data set of resources. This means it is able to be accessed according to defined information models and in a series of different formats for human and machine-readable purposes.

To view this page's information in according to defined information models and in other formats, see the Alternates View of it.
